About Me

In 2016 I met the CTO of a startup company playing DoTA 2 (a MOBA videogame!) and shortly after was offered a job in their Quality Assurance department. After a few years of working as a QA engineer I became the techical writer for the company. While I enjoyed my work I knew I was up for something more challenging, and I decided to explore software engineering as a potential career path!
